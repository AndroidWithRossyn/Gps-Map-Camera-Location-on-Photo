GeoClick: GPS Map Camera – Capture Photos & Videos with Location Tags

GPS Map Camera is a feature-rich Android app that enables users to capture high-quality photos and record videos with real-time GPS location stamps. Whether you are a traveler, real estate agent, surveyor, or field worker, this app ensures your images and videos contain accurate location details, making them ideal for professional and personal use.

Key Features:

  • Geotag Photos & Videos: Automatically embed latitude, longitude, altitude, and address details into images and videos.
  • Map Overlay: Display a real-time map on your photos to show the precise capture location.
  • Customizable Watermark: Choose from different map styles, text sizes, and colors for location stamps.
  • Date & Time Stamp: Add current date and time to your images for accurate record-keeping.
  • EXIF Metadata Support: Store GPS coordinates directly in image properties for easy reference.
  • Multiple Coordinate Formats: Supports Decimal, DMS, and UTM formats.
  • Easy Sharing: Share geotagged photos and videos instantly via social media, email, or messaging apps.
  • Professional Use: Ideal for real estate photography, property verification, travel blogging, fieldwork, and more.
